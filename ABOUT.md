# About Fursal

## What is Fursal?

Fursal is a comprehensive venue booking platform designed to simplify the process of finding and reserving futsal grounds. Whether you're organizing a casual game with friends or managing a professional venue, Fursal connects players with quality futsal facilities through an intuitive, map-based interface.

## The Problem We Solve

Finding and booking futsal venues has traditionally been a hassle:
- Calling multiple venues to check availability
- Uncertainty about pricing and facilities
- Lack of transparency in the booking process
- Manual payment verification causing delays
- No centralized platform to compare venues

Fursal eliminates these pain points by bringing everything online in one seamless platform.

## How It Works

### For Players

**1. Discover Venues**
- Open the app and see all nearby futsal grounds on an interactive map
- Browse venues with real-time availability, pricing, and ratings
- Filter by location, price range, and user reviews

**2. Book Instantly**
- Select your preferred venue and time slot
- See exactly what you're paying for with transparent pricing
- Complete secure payment through eSewa in minutes

**3. Play & Review**
- Receive instant booking confirmation
- Show up and play at your reserved time
- Share your experience through ratings and reviews

### For Venue Owners

**1. List Your Venue**
- Create a compelling venue profile with photos and details
- Set your pricing and availability schedule
- Manage multiple venues from one dashboard

**2. Manage Bookings**
- View all bookings in real-time
- Automatic payment verification through eSewa
- Track revenue and booking analytics

**3. Grow Your Business**
- Reach more players through our platform
- Build reputation with user reviews
- Optimize pricing based on demand insights

### For Administrators

**1. Platform Oversight**
- Manage venue owners and user accounts
- Monitor platform-wide analytics
- Ensure quality standards across all venues
