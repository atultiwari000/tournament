# Fursal User Guide

Welcome to Fursal! This guide will help you navigate and make the most of our futsal venue booking platform.

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Visit the Fursal website and click **Sign Up** or **Register**
2. Choose your registration method:
   - **Google Sign-In**: Quick one-click registration with your Google account
   - **Email/Password**: Create an account with your email address and a secure password
3. Complete your profile with basic information
4. You're ready to start booking!

### Signing In

- Click **Sign In** from the homepage
- Use your registered credentials (Google or email/password)
- Access your personalized dashboard

## Finding the Perfect Venue

### Browse Venues

When you first arrive at Fursal, you'll see an interactive map displaying all available futsal grounds in your area.

**Map Features:**
- **Location Markers**: Each venue is marked on the map
- **Venue Cards**: Click any marker to see venue details including name, rating, and price
- **Current Location**: Allow location access to see venues near you

### Search & Filter

Narrow down your options using our powerful search tools:

- **Search by Name**: Type the venue name in the search bar
- **Location Filter**: Find venues in specific areas or neighborhoods
- **Price Range**: Set your budget and see matching venues
- **Rating Filter**: View only highly-rated venues (e.g., 4+ stars)
- **Availability**: Filter by date and time to see only available slots

### View Venue Details

Click on any venue to see:
- **Photo Gallery**: Browse through venue images
- **Location Map**: Exact location with directions
- **Pricing**: Hourly rates and any special offers
- **Facilities**: Available amenities (lighting, parking, changing rooms, etc.)
- **Availability Calendar**: Real-time slot availability
- **Reviews & Ratings**: Feedback from other users
- **Manager Contact**: Get in touch for special requests

## Making a Booking

### Step-by-Step Booking Process

1. **Select a Venue**: Choose your preferred futsal ground

2. **Pick Date & Time**:
   - Use the calendar to select your booking date
   - View available time slots for that day
   - Select your desired slot(s)

3. **Review Booking Details**:
   - Confirm venue, date, and time
   - Check the total price
   - Review any booking policies

4. **Proceed to Payment**:
   - Click **Confirm & Pay**
   - You have 5 minutes to complete the payment (your slot is held during this time)

5. **Complete Payment via eSewa**:
   - You'll be redirected to eSewa payment gateway
   - Log in to your eSewa account
   - Confirm the payment amount
   - Complete the transaction

6. **Payment Confirmation**:
   - Upon successful payment, you'll be redirected back to Fursal
   - Your booking is automatically confirmed
   - Receive instant confirmation on screen

### Understanding Booking Status

Your bookings can have different statuses:

- **Pending**: Payment in progress (5-minute window)
- **Confirmed**: Payment successful, booking secured
- **Completed**: You've played at the venue (after your booking time)
- **Cancelled**: Booking was cancelled before completion

## Managing Your Bookings

### View Booking History

Access all your bookings from your **User Dashboard**:

1. Click on your profile or **My Bookings**
2. See a complete list of:
   - **Upcoming Bookings**: Future reservations
   - **Past Bookings**: Completed games
   - **Cancelled Bookings**: Previous cancellations

Each booking shows:
- Venue name and location
- Date and time slot
- Payment amount
- Current status
- Booking reference number

### Cancel a Booking

If you need to cancel:
- Cancellations must be made before the payment verification
- Contact the venue manager through their provided contact details for refund requests
- Refund policies vary by venue

## Reviews & Ratings

### Leave a Review

After completing a booking, share your experience:

1. Go to **My Bookings** and find your completed booking
2. Click **Write a Review** or **Rate Venue**
3. Give a star rating (1-5 stars)
4. Write your review describing:
   - Venue condition and cleanliness
   - Facilities and amenities
   - Overall experience
   - Any recommendations

### Edit or Delete Reviews

- Return to your review anytime
- Click **Edit** to update your feedback
- Or select **Delete** to remove your review

## Profile Management

### Update Your Profile

Keep your information current:

1. Click on your **Profile** or account icon
2. Update:
   - Name and contact information
   - Email address
   - Phone number
   - Profile picture

### Change Password

For email/password accounts:
1. Go to **Profile Settings**
2. Select **Change Password**
3. Enter your current password
4. Set a new secure password
5. Confirm the change

### Reset Forgotten Password

If you forget your password:
1. Click **Forgot Password** on the sign-in page
2. Enter your registered email
3. Check your email for reset instructions
4. Follow the link to create a new password

## Payment & Refunds

### Payment Methods

- **eSewa**: Secure digital wallet payment
- All transactions are encrypted and secure
- Real-time payment confirmation

### Refund Process

If a booking cannot be completed (venue issues, double booking, etc.):

1. The venue manager will review your case
2. If approved, refund is processed to your account
3. Manager provides refund confirmation
4. You'll be notified of the refund status
5. Funds typically return within 3-5 business days

### Payment Issues

If you encounter payment problems:
- Contact the venue manager using provided details
- Check your eSewa transaction history
- Screenshot any error messages
- Reach out to Fursal support through the contact page

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Can't find a venue?**
- Enable location services for better results
- Try broadening your search area
- Check your filter settings

**Payment failed?**
- Verify eSewa account has sufficient balance
- Check your internet connection
- Try again after a few minutes
- Contact eSewa support if issue persists

**Booking not showing?**
- Refresh your booking history page
- Check your email for confirmation
- Contact venue manager with payment proof

**Map not loading?**
- Refresh the page
- Clear browser cache
- Check internet connection
- Try a different browser
